Ian Schambach Family Lineage

Source: shambaugh.org — a genealogical archive of the Schambach / Shambaugh family maintained by family members. This archive documents a subset of that record.

This archive traces the direct line of the Schambach family from Hermann Schambach (~1575, Bad Hersfeld, Germany) to Jakob Schambach (b. 1843, Bensheim, Germany) — nine generations spanning approximately 270 years, from the Landgraviate of Hesse-Kassel to the Rhine region. From Jakob, the full branching descendant tree is documented through to the most recent recorded families.
